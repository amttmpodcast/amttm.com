Uncut Gems: The Sublimity of Futility

29 May 2020

The Safdie Brothers' sui generis film, Uncut Gems, is our choice of work this week. We give our best interpretations of the crazy life of Howard Ratner, discuss how the Safdie Brothers make us feel so anxious and delve into the puzzling ideas of Lovecraft's cosmicism and 'Dharma' movies.
