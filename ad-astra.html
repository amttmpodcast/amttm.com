Ad Astra: Can We Go Too Far?

03 July 2020

While most would agree space is the final frontier, James Gray's Ad Astra says otherwise. This film is imbued with ideas entirely contradictory to Nolan's popular work Interstellar, questioning the validity of deep space travel and its impact on the human psyche. Join us as we discuss these ideas as well as the not-so-perfect science in this pensive film.
