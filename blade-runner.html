Blade Runner: Defining Humanity

10 July 2020

This time, we attack two films at once - the Blade Runner duology. We discuss what it means to be human, hypothesising the requirements for being 'conscious'. We also explore the long history of the different editions of the original film as well as its controversial love scene between Harrison Ford and Sean Young.
