Schindler's List: A Cinematic Legend

12 March 2021

Schindler's List will forever exist in the hall of fame of cinema. This film explores a delicate topic perfectly, presenting all the sides of humanity. We explore Goeth, one of the best villains in contemporary film, the redemption arc of Schindler and the accurate portrayal of Nazi bureaucracy in this legendary film.
