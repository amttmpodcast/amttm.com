Breaking Bad Edition

An episodic breakdown of the entire Breaking Bad series, the best TV show on the planet

16 January 2021

Breaking Bad: A Prelude

Welcome to the Breaking Bad Edition of A Method to the Madness, an episodic review and discussion of the magnificent series following a high school teacher turned drug kingpin. We introduce why we're doing this, the series as a whole and how you can join us on this journey.
