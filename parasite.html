Parasite: Oscar-Worthy?

22 May 2020

This time, we discuss Bong Joon-ho's Parasite, debating whether it truly deserves its four Oscars and offering better alternatives for the awardee of the 2019 Academy Award for Best Picture. We also discuss the capitalist ideals in Parasite and Patrick reveals his theory on the powerful ineffability of films in its conveyance of emotions and ideas, creating the term knowledge simulation.
